My Top 3 Fav Movies

1) 3 Idiots

3 Idiots is a 2009 Indian coming-of-age comedy-drama film co-written, produced and directed by Rajkumar Hirani. It is loosely adapted from the novel Five Point Someone by Chetan Bhagat. The film stars Aamir Khan, R. Madhavan, Sharman Joshi, Kareena Kapoor, Boman Irani and Omi Vaidya. The story follows the friendship of three students at an Indian engineering college and is a satire about the social pressures under the Indian education system.

2) The Pursuit of Happiness

The Pursuit of Happiness is a 2006 American biographical drama film directed by Gabriele Muccino and starring Will Smith as Chris Gardner, a homeless salesman. The film is based on the true story of Gardner's nearly one-year struggle with homelessness. The film was released on December 15, 2006, and received positive reviews from critics. Smith's performance was widely praised, earning him an Academy Award nomination for Best Actor.

3) The Shawshank Redemption

The Shawshank Redemption is a 1994 American drama film written and directed by Frank Darabont, based on the 1982 Stephen King novella Rita Hayworth and Shawshank Redemption. The film stars Tim Robbins as Andy Dufresne and Morgan Freeman as Ellis "Red" Redding. It tells the story of Andy's imprisonment for a crime he didn't commit and his friendship with Red, set against the backdrop of the Shawshank State Penitentiary. The film explores themes of hope, friendship, and redemption.
